My Work

I've assembled a collection of academic and professional work that I believe best reflects my skills. Please have a look below.

object. is a lifestyle brand created around the idea that a product only has meaning in the context of a person using it. The first product, object.01, is designed specifically to fit in the many contexts of the independent worker. It has three primary features: a compartment for work essentials, a compartment for personal items, It also has a modular style that shifts between backpack and briefcase. object.01 transitions seamlessly from coffee shops to client meetings and everywhere in between.

In the summer of 2016, I worked for the software design and development consultancy projekt202. During my tenure, I had the opportunity to co-lead ethnographic research and experience strategy for a global computer hardware manufacturer with annual revenues of over $50 billion.
